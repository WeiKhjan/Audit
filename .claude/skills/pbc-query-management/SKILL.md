## Query Management Workflow

1. **Raise** - Document query with W/P reference, description, date
2. **Track** - Update status (Open/Pending/Closed)
3. **Follow up** - Set deadlines, escalate unresolved items
4. **Resolve** - Record client response, close with resolution date
5. **Summarize** - Include in completion memo for unresolved items

## Going Concern PBC Items

The following PBC items are MANDATORY when going concern indicators are identified:

| No | Document | Priority | Required for WP Ref | Purpose |
|----|----------|----------|-------------------|---------|
| 1 | **Director's Written Support Letter** | CRITICAL | F1, F7 | Written undertaking to continue financial support and not demand repayment |
| 2 | **Cash Flow Projections** | HIGH | F1 | Management's forward-looking cash flow forecast (minimum 12 months) |
| 3 | **Post-YE Bank Statements** | HIGH | F1, F2, C9 | Verify post-YE cash position and activity |
| 4 | **Post-YE Management Accounts** | HIGH | F1, F2 | Subsequent financial performance data |
| 5 | **Business Plan / Strategy Document** | MEDIUM | F1, A3 | Management's plans for the business |
| 6 | **Loan/Facility Correspondence** | MEDIUM | F1, D5 | Status of borrowing facilities, covenant compliance |
| 7 | **Director's Personal Financial Statement** | MEDIUM | F1 | Evidence of director's ability to provide financial support |
| 8 | **Minutes Post-YE** | MEDIUM | F1, F2 | Board discussions on going concern |

## PBC Follow-Up and Escalation Framework

### Escalation Timeline

| Day | Action | Responsible | Documentation |
|-----|--------|-------------|---------------|
| **Day 0** | PBC request sent to client | Audit team | PBC request letter with deadline |
| **Day 7** | First follow-up (email/call) | Audit team | Follow-up email documented |
| **Day 14** | Second follow-up + escalate to engagement partner | Audit manager | Escalation email to partner |
| **Day 21** | Formal letter to client directors | Engagement partner | Written notice of outstanding items |
| **Day 28+** | Assess scope limitation impact | Engagement partner | Document in F8 and impact on report |

### Impact Assessment for Outstanding PBC

When PBC items remain outstanding at completion stage, document:

| Outstanding Item | WP Affected | Can Alternative Procedures Be Performed? | Impact on Conclusion | Impact on Audit Report |
|-----------------|------------|----------------------------------------|---------------------|----------------------|
| [PBC item] | [WP ref] | [Yes — describe / No] | [Can conclude / Cannot conclude] | [None / Scope limitation / Qualified / Disclaimer] |

### Scope Limitation Decision Framework

| Scenario | Response |
|----------|---------|
| PBC item received late but before completion | Complete procedures; no report impact |
| PBC item not received; alternative procedures successful | Document alternative procedures; no report impact |
| PBC item not received; alternatives insufficient; immaterial | Document limitation; no report modification |
| PBC item not received; alternatives insufficient; material but not pervasive | Qualified opinion (scope limitation) |
| PBC item not received; alternatives insufficient; material and pervasive | Disclaimer of opinion |
